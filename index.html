Features

Marina Mapping

See who is in your marina with a quick glance at the home page map.

Store Customer Details

Keep track of customer contact details like phone numbers and email addresses.

Print Invoices & Receipts

When a customer wants to leave you can generate a PDF invoice & receipt for them. Details of their payment is recorded into a secure databases.


Keep Track of Arrivals

Keep track of all bookings and see when your visitors are going to arrive

Keep Track of Finances

Keep track of who has paid and who still owes money with our accounts overview page.

Create Mailing Lists

Get a list of all emails for current boats in marina or for all accounts on the system. Useful for mailing updates to all users. Note: Always get permission before sending marketing emails to customers.
